Active Projects

Anomaly Detection

LeJEPA-AD

Architecture-agnostic anomaly detection using bidirectional latent-space predictors. A forward predictor maps 8 context patches to 1 center patch for structural anomalies, while a backward predictor maps 1 center to 8 context patches for logical and positional anomalies. Uses SIGReg regularization for in-domain pretraining.

Currently testing across 14 model architectures under 20M parameters, including FastViT, RepViT, TinyViT, ViT, and ConvNeXt V2.

Benchmarking

Continual Anomaly Detection Benchmarking

Exposed reproducibility problems in the continual anomaly detection (CLAD) field. Demonstrated performance collapses under realistic distribution drift, with AUROC dropping from 92.4% to 52.6%. Developed DINOCore, achieving 0.969 AUROC on MVTec-AD.
